About Us

We established MiCa & Co. TRAVEL AND TOURS to share our passion for adventure, exploration, and fun! Each of our founders renewed their purpose in life while traveling with the people they love. We want to share this magic with local and international tourists through the services only MiCa & Co. TRAVEL AND TOURS can provide.

Mission

We are committed to providing bespoke, reliable, and accessible travel services for worry-free vacations to any destination.

MiCa & Co. Travel is your dedicated partner in delivering tailored and trustworthy travel services, ensuring worry-free vacations to any destination. With a passionate team at the helm, we meticulously customize every element of your journey to align with your desires. From seamless logistics to personalized experiences, we're here to make your dream vacation a reality. Experience the world with confidence, guided by MiCa & Co.'s commitment to excellence.

Vision

At MiCa & Co. Travel, our goal is to become a trusted travel and tours agency in Manila known for providing personalized and memorable experiences to both local and international tourists.

We are dedicated to delivering reliable and well-crafted journeys that reflect our commitment to quality. By focusing on creating authentic and meaningful travel experiences, we aim to build a reputation based on trust and customer satisfaction. Our team works diligently to ensure that every traveler enjoys a unique and enjoyable adventure, leaving with cherished memories of their time with us.
